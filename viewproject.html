Overview

This web application features an optimized vehicle management structure and customized catalog system crafted to display elite high-performance vehicles. It manages garage inventory logs, structures multi-tier specifications for prospective clients, and processes simulation filters; utilizing MVC architecture patterns, secured dynamic parameters, well-arranged grid structures, and comprehensive data controls for a precise tracking workflow.

Role

Full Stack Developer

Core Features

Customer & Vehicle Management

Centralized CRUD and relationship management for car owners, vehicles, service history, and contact info, including maintenance logs and grouping for billing.

Job Cards & Invoice Management

Generate and maintain job cards, charge schedules for parts and labor, adjustments, and statements; supports recurring maintenance plans and manual billing entries.

Payments & Billing Reconciliation

Accept and record payments, apply payments to specific service invoices, handle refunds and item voids, and reconcile daily deposits against garage ledgers.

Parts Inventory & Transactions

Manage spare parts inventory and categories, create sales transactions with itemized line items, and track parts utilization, stock alerts, and shop income.

Service Reports & Analytics

Produce shop revenue statements, customer account summaries, and mechanic operational reports; export PDFs/Excel for financial audits and garage management review.

Activity Logs & Service Notifications

Record logs for vehicle repair updates, send automated email or SMS notifications for completed jobs and receipts, and run background tasks for service reminders.
